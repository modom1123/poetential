Product Manager — data platforms & internal tooling

Data only drives better care when the people delivering that care can actually use it. Most internal platforms are built for the people who built them — not the clinicians, coordinators, and operators who need answers. Bridging that gap is the work I do.

Why Equip

Equip's ability to scale depends on more than clinical excellence. It depends on the teams behind it — therapists, care coordinators, operations leads — having reliable access to the information that shapes every decision they make. That problem, getting the right data to the right people in a form they can actually act on, is where I've built my career. It's also the most important infrastructure problem a growing care organization can solve.

The work

IMPLAN

Senior Product Manager — internal platform

Built a 0-to-1 internal operational platform for an organization whose users — economists, policy analysts, and planners — needed complex data to drive real-world decisions. The core challenge wasn't technical: it was designing a system that non-technical users could trust and navigate independently, reducing reliance on the data team to answer questions the platform should answer itself.

Leadventure

Senior Product Manager — Tealium CDP & data governance

Owned a customer data platform that sat at the center of how marketing and operations teams collected, structured, and acted on data across the organization. The real work was downstream: ensuring the data reaching business stakeholders was clean, reliable, and usable without a data engineer in the room — the same self-serve problem, applied to a different domain.
